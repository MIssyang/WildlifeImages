Sid

Obsidian "Sid" (presumed male) came to Wildlife Images on June 30, 2008 along with his sibling. On arrival he had a broken leg, but he and his sibling were otherwise healthy. Sid’s leg never healed well due to the fact that he was still a nestling and had to learn to stand and walk while his leg was in a sling. Due to his delayed physical healing, his mental development as a crow was also delayed.

It was also noted during his leg operation that he had permanent wing damage, which prevented him from flying. His flight issues made him unable to be a candidate for release. Sid however, spends the spring time as a foster dad to all the juvenile crows that come to our facility for rehabilitation, teaching them the ways of the crow.
