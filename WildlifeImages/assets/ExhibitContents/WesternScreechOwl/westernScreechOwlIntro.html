Western Screech Owl Intro

Van Gough (Presumed Male) - Arrived at Wildlife Images on October 8, 2010.
Van Gough was found by a concerned citizen driving on Elk Creek Rd. who found him sitting in the road. She stopped to pick him up & found that Van Gough had only one eye. She brought him to our facility to receive care. Upon exam, several tears were found in his ear. Due to this & his missing eye unfortunately, Van Gough could not be released. He has become a great addition to our educational program.
