Western Screech Owl

Western Screech Owls are a common small owl of the West and can be found in urban, suburban parks and residential areas, as well as wild lands. They live in a diversity of habitats, but associate with riparian habitats and deciduous trees. Occasionally one takes prey larger than itself, including cottontail rabbits and Mallards. Western Screech-Owls are vulnerable to habitat loss because of urban development. Their diet consists mainly of Mammals, birds, worms, insects, and crayfish.
