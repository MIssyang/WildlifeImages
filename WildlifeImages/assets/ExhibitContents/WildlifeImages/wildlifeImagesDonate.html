Support

Did you know that Wildlife Images is totally unfunded by the state or federal government? We rely solely on contributions from individuals and organizations just like you to carry out our dual mission of rehabilitation and education. Every little bit that we receive is tremendously appreciated and used to its fullest capacity.

You can provide essential support to Wildlife Images in a multitude of ways ranging from adopting an animal, becoming a member, purchasing stepping stones, making a tax deductible donations, endowment giving, or volunteering your time and services. Learn more at www.wildlifeimages.org/support
